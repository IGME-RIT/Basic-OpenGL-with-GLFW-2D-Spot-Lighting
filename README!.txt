Welcome to the Spot Lighting 2D Tutorial!
Prerequesites: Point Lighting 2D

This is just like the point light, except we are
taking direction and FOV into consideration, and
if something is outside the FOV, then we do not add light
to the pixel, just like how we jumped from point light
to spot light in 3D
